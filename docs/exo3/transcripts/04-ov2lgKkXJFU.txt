# AI Wont Replace People But It Will Replace Meaningless Roles
# 2026-05-26 | ov2lgKkXJFU | 125 words

AI won't replace people, but it will reduce meaningless roles in organizations. Today, for example, in big companies, middle management does nothing but shuffle the deck chairs based on insensitivity from lower down, and then they reassess that to try and make sense for the upper levels. All of that disappears, and I predict that middle management will shrink by about 10x in the next five years as we allocate a lot more of that to AI. Now, this does not mean lack of employment. It means we'll do a lot more, and therefore there'll be lots of work for other people to do. We've seen that repeatedly, also. But AI will completely change and transform useless roles that are currently being done in organizations.
