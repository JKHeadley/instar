# Strategy Is Not the Hard Part Anymore
# 2026-06-04 | Rp7euq7Zwok | 156 words

The hard part today is not strategy. People think often with a company or an organization, what is our strategy? That's not the hard part. The hard part is speed of execution and constantly running a million experiments. Uh Jeff Bezos famously said the success of Amazon is a function of how many experiments we run. Because in a volatile world, you need to be constantly running thousands of experiments to be sensors for what's happening in the in the real world and then making very quick decision-making to react to that new model that was completely different from the way you thought about it even weeks or months ago. If you watch what's happening in AI, it's forcing a change where in the future as we move forward, all strategy will be irrelevant. Because by the time you finish a five-year plan, it's out of date and you're spending all your time maintaining the plan.
