# Consensus Is Becoming a Liability
# 2026-05-31 | ZeacoaPfBPc | 80 words

Consensus is becoming a liability in most of our organizations. Most big companies worship consensus, alignment meetings, cross-functional synchronization, stakeholder buy-in, because consensus feels safe. But in volatile markets, consensus slows down your metabolism. Uh so speed always beats agreement in the world that is coming. Clarity beats harmony. Most companies that win are going to empower smaller groups to act with directional alignment, not universal approval. Because by the time everybody agreed, the opportunity is gone. That's the shift.
