## What Changed

Fixed Telegram recovery queue false-success reporting and a concurrent drain race. The relay verifies the exact attempted-message row before claiming it is queued, and recovery workers now atomically claim, renew, and fence each delivery ID. Empty databases left at historical noncanonical paths are atomically quarantined without deleting evidence.

## What to Tell Your User

Temporary Telegram delivery failures now say “Queued for recovery” only when the recovery record is really durable. If persistence cannot be proved, the failure is stated honestly. Recovery also prevents two workers from sending the same queued reply twice.

## Summary of New Capabilities

- Verify a queued Telegram reply by its exact delivery ID before reporting success.
- Enforce one recovery-drain owner per delivery ID.
- Quarantine known zero-byte legacy queue files while preserving all evidence.

## Evidence

- Unit, integration, and end-to-end recovery tests pass.
- A two-reader race test proves exactly one recovered send.
- A blocked canonical path test proves the shell never claims an unpersisted queue entry.
