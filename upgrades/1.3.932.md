## What Changed

After three consecutive network-level `fetch failed` errors, the Slack API
client replaces its own undici dispatcher and retries through a fresh
connection pool. Successful calls reset the failure streak. The recovery pool
is scoped to Slack requests only — unrelated HTTP clients are not affected.
`undici` is now a declared dependency (it was previously imported as a
transitive package).

## What to Tell Your User

After a laptop network change (new Wi-Fi, hotspot, VPN flip), outbound Slack
delivery now recovers on its own within a few failed attempts instead of
staying dead until a server restart. Nothing to configure.

## Summary of New Capabilities

- Slack outbound transport self-heals after network interface changes.
- Recovery is scoped to the Slack client's own connection pool.
